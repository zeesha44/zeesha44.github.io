Uniting Muslim Education - We are committed to fostering unity of idea and co-operation among private Islamically oriented Nursery, Primary and Secondary Schools in Nigeria.

Our Mission

We engage in formulation and implementation of programmes and policies aimed at assisting the regulation, growth and improvement of standards in member schools. That's not just all, we also serve as a mouthpiece for member schools in relation to government.

What We Offer

School Directory

Directory of the entire member schools of AMIS can be registered in the portal. This enables the general public nationwide to easily locate and enroll their children.

Educational Standards

We maintain high educational standards through regular monitoring and support for member schools.

Support Network

Access to a network of Islamic schools and educational resources to enhance learning outcomes.
