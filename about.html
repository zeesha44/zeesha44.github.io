About AMIS Nigeria

Association of Model Islamic Schools Nigeria (AMIS) is committed to fostering unity of idea and co-operation among private Islamically oriented Nursery, Primary and Secondary Schools in Nigeria.

We engage in formulation and implementation of programmes and policies aimed at assisting the regulation, growth and improvement of standards in member schools. We also serve as a mouthpiece for member schools in relation to government.
